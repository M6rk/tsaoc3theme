Frequently Asked Questions

Our main worship service is held every Sunday at 10:30 AM. We welcome people of all ages and backgrounds to join us for a time of worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth. The service typically lasts about an hour and includes contemporary music, prayer, and a message from God's Word.

Yes! We have Kelsa Kids, a wonderful children's program that runs during our Sunday morning worship service. Children participate in age-appropriate activities, games, songs, and Bible stories that help them learn about God's love in fun and creative ways. All children are welcome, and our trained volunteers ensure a safe and nurturing environment.

There are many ways to get involved! We offer volunteer opportunities in our community services programs, including food assistance, clothing distribution, and support for families in need. You can also participate in our special events and fundraising activities throughout the year. Contact our church office or speak with one of our pastoral team members to learn more about how you can make a difference in our community.

Ways We Help

Community Assistance

Our Community Life Centre is a safe haven – a place where you can find hope. When you call for appointment you will meet with a caseworker. Our caseworkers are great listeners – they know it takes time to work through each situation. Each situation is unique and we create an individual plan to meet your needs. We love to walk alongside your journey.

Learn More

Programs

Our programs are designed to help people with life. We have classes such as budgeting and parenting programs. Practical hands-on skills that everyone needs. Plus weekly opportunities to meet others in our community. A variety of Bible Studies are also offered.

Learn More

Thrift Stores

Our Thrift Stores (200 Rutland Rd. South and 3531 Old Okanagan Hwy #100) generate funds for our programs. When you donate gently used items and purchase items from the store you help us to live our motto Giving Help Today.

Learn More

Emergency Services

Salvation Army Emergency Disaster Services Volunteers work alongside emergency first responders and other agencies to provide relief and comfort to those in crisis. We are often the ones serving a meal to firefighters or providing snacks and water at an Emergency Reception Centre.

Learn More

Christmas

We love Christmas. It is a time to offer families in our community with a Christmas Blessing. During the month of November families can register for our program. During the Christmas Season there are many ways the community can be involved as well.

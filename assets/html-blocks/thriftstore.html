Thrift Stores

The Salvation Army Thrift Stores generate funds to support our programs by efficiently and ethically recycling and selling donated clothing and other goods. We exceed our customers' expectations while providing an environment that is beneficial to the development and well-being of both our paid and unpaid mission partners.

Our Thrift Stores are blessed with a dedicated and faithful team of staff and volunteers.

We look forward to seeing you soon!

New Vista Ministries

New Vista Ministries exists to provide a specific ministry to the whole person (body, mind and spirit) for adults 55+ within our church and community.

Seniors' Drop-In

When & Where

Wednesdays: 10:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.

Season: September to June

Location: Church Gym

Cost: $4.00

The Seniors' Drop-In runs on Wednesdays (10:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.) from September to June in the church gym. Come early to build friendships and play some board games. Our amazing team of kitchen volunteers prepares a healthy soup and sandwich lunch each week.

Musical Entertainment & Educational Workshops

Program runs from 11:45 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.

Special programs at Thanksgiving, Christmas, Valentine's & Easter

Each week, a unique program is offered. A variety of musical groups and soloists from across the Central Okanagan area, many of whom are also senior adults, offer their talents to benefit those attending the Drop-In. You're guaranteed to have a foot-tapping good time! You may even find yourself getting up and dancing. Some weeks, educational or information workshops of interest to senior adults are offered.

We make every effort to keep connected with and care for Drop-In attendees who become sick or shut in through telephone calls, e-mails or personal visits to homes and hospitals. We also regularly visit past members who can no longer attend due to ill health.

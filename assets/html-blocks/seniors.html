New Vista Seniors Program

New Vista Seniors Program exists to provide a ministry to the whole person (body, mind, and soul) for adults 55+ within our church and community.

When & Where

Wednesdays: 11:30 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.

Season: September to June (a Christmas break from mid November to mid January)

Location: Church Gym

Cost: $4.00

Come early to have coffee/tea and goodies, build friendships and meet new people. Our amazing team of kitchen volunteers prepare a healthy soup, sandwich and dessert lunch for everyone to enjoy.

Musical Entertainment & Educational Workshops

Program runs from 11:30 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.

Special programs at Christmas and Easter

Each week a unique program is offered. The program may include:

  • A variety of musical/entertainment groups or individuals - many of whom are also senior adults
  • A variety of speakers with presentations on topics of interest that may include lifestyle, humor, hobbies, human interest, health, protection, safety & services for seniors.

We make every effort to keep connected with and care for Drop-In attendees who become sick or shut in through telephone calls, e-mails or personal visits to homes and hospitals. We also regularly visit past members who can no longer attend due to ill health.

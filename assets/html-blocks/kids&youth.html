Kids & Youth

Kids

Okanagan Central Community Church loves children. During the Sunday Gathering, children ages 2 to 12 are invited to attend children's church during the adult sermon.




"Let the children come to me. Don't stop them! For the Kingdom of Heaven belongs to those who are like these children."

Jesus

Youth

Okanagan Central Community Church seeks to provide a place where youth can discover and develop a deep, meaningful relationship with Jesus Christ. We also want them to seek to find joy in service to the Church and the community.

"…this is what he [the LORD] requires of you: to do what is right, to love mercy, and to walk humbly with your God."

Micah 6:8b (NLT)

Emergency Services

The unpredictability of a disaster leaves us all vulnerable.

There are many within our community who have experienced the sudden loss that accompanies disaster. At those times it is an army of volunteers that rises to come alongside the survivors and to help diminish the feelings of helplessness and hopelessness.

In the Central Okanagan, we are privileged to work alongside emergency first responders and other agencies to provide relief and comfort. Whether it is the feeding of first responders on the scene or giving care and comfort in the reception centre, EDS volunteers are prepared to help in whatever way possible.

Our highly-trained volunteers work in teams to provide a well-disciplined and well-rounded approach to helping with a disaster. Our teams respond from their hearts and give hope even when it seems that all is lost.

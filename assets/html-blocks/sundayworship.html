What can I expect and other FAQ's...

What Can I expect?

Come expecting to meet Jesus! Through our prayers and songs and a relevant message from the Bible you will have the opportunity to meet with the Lord and to worship him. Whether you are a long time believer or just exploring what Christianity is, you are welcome.

How Early Should I Get There?

Our service starts at 10:30. Most people arrive anywhere from 10:00 to 10:30. If you discover that the parking lot is full, the lot across the road (beside Valorosa), is our overflow parking; feel free to use it.

What should I wear?

You'll find people in jeans, skirts, shorts, as well as suits. We believe what's on the inside of a person is more important than what is worn on the outside.

What do I need to know about Giving Money?

Tithing is an expression of thanksgiving and offering as part of Worship. This helps with our ability to offer church ministry. If you are new to our church we do not expect you to tithe. For regular attenders you can tithe via the offering plate or you can set up direct deposit using this form.

OCCC Authorization Form for Contributions & Donations 2020

If you'd like to be receipted for your tithe please put your name and address on a tithe envelope. We will send Direct Deposit receipts directly to you.

What if I want to learn more?

Talk to a pastor after worship, or give us a phone call!

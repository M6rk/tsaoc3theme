Men's Ministry

"We proclaim Him, admonishing every man and teaching every man with all wisdom, so that we may present every man complete in Christ."

Colossians 1:28

We have been challenged to be a true disciple by: Loving, living, serving, giving, and then passing that on to the next generation. Come and grow…then be ready to give back!

All men are invited, regardless of their stage of life. Men's ministry also includes a yearly Men's Camp and Men's Breakfasts.
